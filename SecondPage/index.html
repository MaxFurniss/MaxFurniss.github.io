BMW i8

By Car Reviews For You

What- What is it?

The BMW I8 is only BWM’s second sports car, you would think that after making two exceptional cars, what the heck they have been doing this time. It is extremely high tech and modern.

Why- Why is it Unique and Modern?

For starters it is a hybrid, which spells environment friendly and modern better than anything else, it also works a charm as a sports car and a luxury car, you get sumptuous comfort and speed! It also has a really cool system that you can use to control the car from your phone! It also is very affordable, put all this together then the BMW i8 really is from ‘another world’
