Porsche 918 Spyder

By Car Reviews For You

What- What is it?

The Porsche 918 Spyder is the definition of brilliance. It is by far the best car Porsche have ever made. It is Porsche’s answer to the monster supercars of the year. It is the first real heavy competition to the McLaren P1 and Ferrari’s LA Ferrari.

Drive- What's it like to drive?

The 918 is like a camouflage when it comes to driving, it adapts to the environment it is in perfectly. In the city it is comfortable, cool, quiet and electric. On the motorway it is smooth and manageable. On the country roads it is secure, immediate, and massively confidence-inspiring; racetracks: razor sharp, suffers no fools and finally once released on the it really does come into a world of its own, it drives like it’s never going to drive again. It’s an intoxicating package, and so rewarding and absorbing to spend time with.
