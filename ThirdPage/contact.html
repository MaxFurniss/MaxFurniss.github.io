Chevrolet Corvette Equipment

BMW i8 Performance Data Recorder

Record, share and analyze your driving experiences on and off the track with the industry-exclusive Performance Data Recorder1. Add an extra dimension to your drive by logging video of your drives, along with real-time performance data, on an SD memory card stored in the glove box. Four modes — Track, Sport, Touring and Performance — allow you to capture video, audio, driving stats, date and time, and a lot more.

BMW i8 Intelligent interior design

Stingray’s interior space is pure and dynamic, with a single sweeping contour unifying instrument console and panel, and door trim. The smaller, 360mm super-tight steering wheel gives you a more direct, race-track feel.

Connecting you closely with the car’s performance, an 8” high-definition touch-screen displays key data and configures automatically when you switch drive mode.

BMW i8 Attention-grabbing exterior

Stingray’s breathtaking, streamlined form is race-track and wind-tunnel proven. Five air vents in the bonnet, front bumper and rear, boost the car’s powerful aura, cool the engine and reduce lift. Frosted high- intensity LED headlamps and four-element tail lights flash your advance; iconic Corvette Stingray badges sign your arrival.

BMW i8 Maximise your smart-phone

New for model-year 2016, the Stingray provides smarter, safer, more complete in-car use of your smart-phone. Simply control everything using the cabin’s touch-screen display. Compatibility is offered with both: Apple CarPlay®: effortlessly make calls, send and receive messages, listen to music, and run third-party apps – including via Siri voice commands

Android Auto®: talk to Google, and access scores of apps including Google Maps, Google Now, Google Play Music, Spotify, WhatsApp, and Skype2 Also new are dual cameras mounted in the front bumper. These combine to project a birds-eye view of the car’s nose onto the touch-screen, helping you park perfectly every time.

BMW i8 Sensual interior finish

Inside Stingray’s cockpit, premium materials and crafted finish accelerate your pleasure. Panels and steering wheel are hand-wrapped and stitched in sensuous leather or suede-effect micro-fibre; hard surfaces are in gleaming aluminium or carbon-fibre. Bolstered seats, with performance-boosting lightweight magnesium frame, cradle you perfectly.
